COMPANY PROFILE

Chin Ching Enterprise Co., Ltd. has been specializing in the design and manufacturing of stainless steel products automatic welding, stamping & compression in Taiwan since 1982.

Our team of highly dedicated and experienced professionals, along with modernized manufacturing capabilities, has established the company as a reliable manufacturer of stainless steel elbow and high vacuum thermo pot of the highest quality and the role model among peers. Our aim is to develop and produce highest quality products for industrial parts & accessories.

We heartily welcome your prototypes, samples, drawings 2D in .DWG, .DXF, .PDF file and 3D in .IGES and/or .IGS file formation with detailed dimensions, tolerance, specifications, technical data and required stainless steel material, piece volume (mm3) and quantity each per shipment for cost estimation, mold fabrication, welding, assembly and package, just like the turn-key production for total solution in one stop.

In order to achieve 100% customer satisfaction and maintain a high level of quality control, our company had invested a considerable amount of capital into the precise manufacturing facilities. In addition, from making of the mold to production of the product, all procedures of manufacturing are supervised restrictively and are done in our factory.
